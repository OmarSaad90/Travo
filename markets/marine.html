Marine & Coastal Infrastructure

Pioneering coastal protection and marine construction across the region's shorelines. From erosion control structures to port facilities, we deliver robust engineering solutions that withstand the demanding marine environment.

The Marine & Coastal Challenge

The Middle East's coastal regions face unique challenges including extreme wave conditions, saltwater corrosion, coastal erosion, and the need for specialized marine construction techniques that can withstand harsh marine environments.

Extreme Marine Conditions

Operating in environments with high wave energy, strong currents, and saltwater corrosion requires specialized materials, construction methods, and protective systems.

Coastal Erosion

Protecting valuable coastal infrastructure and properties from erosion requires engineered solutions that balance environmental considerations with structural integrity.

Marine Access & Navigation

Developing safe and efficient marine access points, harbors, and navigation channels requires specialized expertise in marine engineering and construction.

Our Marine Construction Capabilities

Comprehensive marine and coastal infrastructure solutions delivered through proven methodologies and cutting-edge technology.

Core Services

  • Revetments: Engineered coastal protection systems using rock armor, concrete units, or gabions
  • Breakwaters: Offshore and nearshore structures that create calm harbor conditions
  • Quay Walls: Heavy-duty marine structures for ports and harbors
  • Skiff Beaches: Specialized beach landing areas for small watercraft
  • Dredging: Capital and maintenance dredging for navigation channels and harbor basins

Technical Excellence

  • Wave climate analysis & modeling
  • Coastal sediment transport studies
  • Marine pile driving & foundations
  • Underwater concrete placement
  • Environmental compliance & marine safety management
