Environmental Solutions

Protecting and enhancing the natural environment through specialized soil improvement, comprehensive investigations, and sustainable coastal protection solutions across the Middle East.

The Environmental Challenge

The Middle East's unique environmental conditions require specialized solutions to address soil instability, coastal erosion, and ecological preservation while supporting sustainable development.

Soil Instability

Addressing challenging soil conditions including sabkha, loose sands, and expansive clays that threaten infrastructure stability and require specialized improvement techniques.

Site Characterization

Conducting comprehensive environmental and geotechnical investigations to understand subsurface conditions and mitigate risks before construction begins.

Coastal Vulnerability

Protecting valuable coastal assets from erosion, sea-level rise, and storm surge while maintaining ecological balance and supporting sustainable development.

Our Environmental Capabilities

Comprehensive environmental solutions delivered through advanced technologies and sustainable practices.

Core Services

  • Soil Improvement: Dynamic compaction, vibro-compaction, stone columns, and soil stabilization techniques
  • Geotechnical Investigations: Drilling, sampling, laboratory testing, and in-situ testing services
  • Coastal Protection: Revetments, breakwaters, groynes, and beach nourishment systems
  • Environmental Monitoring: Water quality, air quality, and ecological assessments

Technical Excellence

  • Advanced geophysical investigation methods
  • Numerical modeling and analysis
  • Sustainable coastal engineering practices
  • Environmental impact assessment expertise
  • Regulatory compliance and permitting support
