Our Oilfield Services Scope

Specialized civil and infrastructure services tailored for the demanding requirements of oil and gas exploration and production operations.

Site Development & Infrastructure

  • Drilling Pad Construction: Well pad preparation, leveling, and reinforcement for rig operations
  • Access Roads: Heavy-duty road construction for equipment mobilization and operations
  • Pipeline Support: Trenching, bedding, backfilling, and pipeline route preparation
  • Facility Foundations: Pump stations, tank farms, and processing facility infrastructure

Specialized Services

  • Earthworks & Grading: Large-scale excavation, mass earthworks, and site leveling
  • Drainage Systems: Stormwater management and containment systems for oilfield sites
  • Environmental Protection: Bund walls, containment systems, and environmental safeguards
  • Maintenance Support: Ongoing civil works and infrastructure maintenance services

Our Oilfield Project Delivery Process

A rigorous approach to oilfield infrastructure projects ensuring safety, quality, and on-time delivery in demanding environments.

1

Site Survey & Planning

Comprehensive site assessment, geotechnical investigations, HSE planning, and detailed engineering design coordination.

2

Mobilization & Setup

Equipment mobilization, temporary facilities, safety systems installation, and HSE orientation for all personnel.

3

Site Preparation

Clearing, grading, excavation, and soil stabilization with continuous quality control and environmental monitoring.

4

Infrastructure Construction

Roads, pads, foundations, and utility installations following oil and gas industry standards and specifications.

5

Quality & Safety Verification

Comprehensive testing, inspection, documentation, and client verification before handover to operations.

6

Handover & Support

Project documentation, as-built drawings, warranty support, and ongoing maintenance service options.

Safety & Compliance Standards

Environmental Protection
