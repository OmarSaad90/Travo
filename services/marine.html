Our Marine Construction Scope

Comprehensive marine engineering and coastal protection solutions spanning port infrastructure, harbor works, and maritime facilities.

Harbor & Port Infrastructure

  • Quay Walls & Wharves: Concrete and steel sheet pile structures for port facilities
  • Breakwaters: Rubble mound and caisson breakwaters for harbor protection
  • Jetties & Piers: Piled and solid-fill structures for berthing and mooring
  • Dredging Works: Capital and maintenance dredging for navigation channels

Coastal Protection & Marine Works

  • Revetments: Rock and concrete armor for shoreline stabilization
  • Seawalls: Vertical and sloped protection structures for coastal erosion
  • Marine Bridges: Over-water structures and causeways
  • Submarine Pipelines: Offshore pipeline installation and protection

Our Delivery Process

A systematic approach to marine construction projects ensuring structural integrity, environmental compliance, and operational excellence.

1

Site Investigation

Comprehensive marine surveys including bathymetry, geotechnical studies, and environmental assessments.

2

Engineering Design

Detailed design of marine structures considering wave action, tidal forces, and seismic loads.

3

Material Procurement

Sourcing of marine-grade materials, armor rock, and specialized equipment for offshore works.

4

Marine Construction

Execution of works using specialized marine equipment, barges, and floating plant.

5

Quality Assurance

Rigorous inspection and testing of marine structures and installations.

6

Handover & Maintenance

Comprehensive handover documentation and ongoing maintenance support services.

Quality & Safety Standards

Adherence to international marine construction standards and stringent safety protocols for offshore works.

Technical Standards

  • BS 6349 (Maritime Structures)
  • PIANC and CIRIA guidelines
  • Eurocode for marine structures
  • Local port authority requirements

Safety Protocols

  • Marine safety procedures
  • Diving and underwater work safety
  • Vessel operations compliance
  • Emergency response planning

Environmental Protection

  • Marine ecosystem protection
  • Sediment control measures
  • Oil spill prevention protocols
  • Environmental monitoring
