Our MEP & Electromechanical Scope

Comprehensive building services engineering solutions ensuring optimal performance, efficiency, and compliance for all facility types.

Mechanical Systems

  • HVAC Systems: Central plants, VRF systems, and precision cooling
  • Plumbing Systems: Domestic water, sanitary drainage, and fire protection
  • Fire Protection: Sprinkler systems, fire alarms, and smoke control
  • Fuel & Gas Systems: LPG, natural gas, and specialized gas systems

Electrical & Controls

  • Power Distribution: Main switchgear, transformers, and distribution boards
  • Lighting Systems: Interior, exterior, and emergency lighting solutions
  • Building Automation: BMS, SCADA, and intelligent control systems
  • Low Current Systems: Security, telecommunications, and data networks

Our Delivery Process

A systematic approach to MEP engineering and installation ensuring seamless integration and optimal system performance.

1

Design & Engineering

Comprehensive MEP design including load calculations, system sizing, and BIM coordination.

2

Procurement & Fabrication

Equipment selection, material procurement, and off-site fabrication of MEP components.

3

Installation

Precision installation of all MEP systems with strict quality control and coordination.

4

Testing & Commissioning

Comprehensive system testing, balancing, and commissioning to ensure optimal performance.

5

Integration & Optimization

System integration, automation programming, and performance optimization.

6

Handover & Training

Documentation delivery, operator training, and ongoing maintenance support.

Quality & Compliance Standards

Adherence to international MEP standards and building codes for optimal performance and safety.

Technical Standards

  • ASHRAE standards for HVAC systems
  • NFPA codes for fire protection
  • IEC and NEC electrical standards
  • International Plumbing Codes

Sustainability

  • LEED and Estidama compliance
  • Energy efficiency optimization
  • Sustainable material selection
  • Water conservation systems

Advanced Technologies

  • Building Information Modeling (BIM)
  • Digital twin technology
  • IoT and smart building integration
  • Predictive maintenance systems
