Our Electrical & Power Scope

Comprehensive electrical engineering and power systems solutions spanning transmission, distribution, and specialized applications.

Power Transmission & Distribution

  • Substation Construction: HV/MV substations, switchyards, and control buildings
  • Overhead Lines: Transmission towers, distribution poles, and conductor stringing
  • Underground Cabling: HV cable laying, jointing, and termination works
  • Power Transformers: Installation, commissioning, and maintenance

Electrical Systems & Applications

  • Renewable Energy: Solar farms, wind power integration, and hybrid systems
  • Backup Power: Generator installations, UPS systems, and battery banks
  • Lighting Systems: Street lighting, flood lighting, and specialized illumination
  • Electrical Networks: LV distribution, earthing systems, and protection

Our Delivery Process

A systematic approach to electrical and power projects ensuring safety, reliability, and compliance with international standards.

1

System Design

Comprehensive electrical design including load flow analysis, protection coordination, and equipment specification.

2

Equipment Procurement

Sourcing of certified electrical equipment, transformers, switchgear, and specialized components.

3

Installation

Precision installation of electrical systems with strict adherence to safety protocols and quality standards.

4

Testing & Commissioning

Comprehensive electrical testing, protection relay settings, and system commissioning.

5

Energization

Safe system energization following established switching procedures and safety protocols.

6

Operation & Maintenance

Ongoing maintenance programs, condition monitoring, and technical support services.

Quality & Safety Standards

Adherence to international electrical standards and stringent safety protocols for power systems.

Technical Standards

  • IEC and IEEE standards compliance
  • National Electrical Code (NEC)
  • BS and EN standards for equipment
  • Local utility requirements

Safety Protocols

  • Live working procedures
  • Permit to work systems
  • Electrical safety rules compliance
  • Emergency response planning

Advanced Technologies

  • Smart grid technologies
  • Digital protection systems
  • SCADA and automation
  • Condition monitoring systems
