LEBANON OPERATIONS

Building Lebanon's Future

For over five decades, Travo Group has been a cornerstone of Lebanon's construction and engineering landscape. From our home in Beirut, we've built lasting infrastructure that connects communities, supports economic growth, and reflects our deep commitment to the nation's development.

Through our family of specialized companiesTRAVO, Moudawar-Edde, C.A.T., and Interprowe deliver comprehensive engineering solutions across transportation, marine works, buildings, and technical services. Our Lebanese heritage drives our dedication to excellence, safety, and sustainable development.
