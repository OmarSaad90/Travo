OMAN OPERATIONS

Building Oman's Renaissance

Travo Group has been a proud partner in the Sultanate of Oman's remarkable transformation and development journey. As one of Oman's most comprehensive construction and engineering groups, we operate through ten specialized companies delivering integrated solutions across every major infrastructure sector.

From the ports of Duqm and Salalah to the oilfields of the interior, from highways connecting the nation to marine works protecting its coastsour diverse family of companies brings unmatched expertise, local knowledge, and international standards to support Oman Vision 2040.
