UAE OPERATIONS

Building the Emirates' Vision

Travo Group is proud to contribute to the United Arab Emirates' remarkable growth and development. Operating through our strategic presence in Dubai and Sharjah, we deliver world-class construction and engineering solutions that support the UAE's vision of innovation, sustainability, and excellence.

Through SAROOJ, TRAVO, and SK (Simon Karam), our companies bring decades of regional expertise combined with international standards to serve the dynamic construction market of the Emirates, from infrastructure and commercial developments to specialized technical services.
