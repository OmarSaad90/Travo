Partner Onboarding Process

Our structured onboarding process ensures compliance and sets the foundation for successful collaboration. Follow these steps to become an approved Travo Group trade partner.

Start Registration
1

Prequalification

Compliance Review

3

Contract Setup

Insurance Requirements

General Liability

Minimum coverage of $2,000,000 per occurrence for bodily injury and property damage.

Required for All Partners

Workers' Compensation

Statutory coverage for all employees as required by local jurisdiction regulations.

Required for All Partners

Auto Liability

Minimum $1,000,000 combined single limit for owned, hired, and non-owned vehicles.

Required if Using Vehicles

Additional Insurance Notes

Travo Group must be named as additional insured
30-day notice of cancellation required
Policy must be primary and non-contributory
Certificates must be submitted before project start
Download Insurance Requirements

Compliance Requirements

Documentation

  • Valid business license and registration
  • Tax registration certificates
  • Safety manuals and procedures
  • Quality control plans
  • Environmental compliance records

Personnel Requirements

  • Certified safety officers on site
  • Trained and qualified workforce
  • Background checks for sensitive positions
  • Regular training and certification updates
  • Drug-free workplace compliance
