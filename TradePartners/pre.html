Prequalification Process

Join Travo Group's network of trusted trade partners and access construction opportunities across the Middle East

Prequalification Criteria

To become a prequalified trade partner, your organization must meet the following requirements:

Business Requirements

  • Valid business registration and trade license
  • Minimum 3 years operational experience
  • Proven track record in relevant sector
  • Financial stability and solvency

HSE & Compliance

  • Valid HSE certifications and policies
  • Insurance coverage (liability & workers)
  • Quality management system (ISO preferred)
  • Commitment to ethical business practices
