Building Communities Through Education

At Travo, we believe in fostering growth through strategic educational partnerships that shape the future of engineering and construction.

Adma International School

Partnering with Adma International School to inspire the next generation of engineers and innovators. Through our collaboration, we provide mentorship programs, career guidance, and hands-on learning experiences that bridge classroom theory with real-world application.
  • Student mentorship programs
  • Engineering career workshops
  • Site visit opportunities
  • Scholarship initiatives

Muscat University

Our strategic partnership with Muscat University focuses on advancing research and development in construction technology and sustainable engineering practices. Together, we're shaping the future of the industry through academic excellence and practical innovation.
  • Research & development projects
  • Internship programs
  • Graduate recruitment initiatives
  • Technical knowledge sharing

Our Community Impact

Through our educational partnerships, we're committed to building stronger communities by investing in education, fostering innovation, and creating opportunities for the next generation of professionals. Our collaborative approach ensures that both students and institutions benefit from real-world expertise and industry insights.
