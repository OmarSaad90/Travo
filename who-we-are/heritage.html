Our Heritage Timeline

Legacy

From academic excellence to engineering leadership, discover the key moments that shaped Travo Group into the regional powerhouse it is today.

1966

Academic Foundation

Université Saint-Joseph

Our founder's journey begins with rigorous engineering education at the prestigious Université Saint-Joseph in Beirut, establishing the academic foundation that would define our commitment to excellence.

Université Saint-Joseph, Beirut - 1966

1968

Advanced Specialization

École Nationale des Ponts et Chaussées

Advanced studies at France's most prestigious engineering school for infrastructure and civil works, where the principles of modern construction engineering and project management were mastered.

École des Ponts et Chaussées, Paris - 1968

1974

Foundation in Lebanon

First Engineering Consultancy

Establishing our first engineering consultancy in Lebanon, marking the beginning of what would become a regional construction and engineering powerhouse spanning multiple countries.

Lebanon - First Office 1974

Today

Regional Excellence

4 Countries, 500+ Projects

Today, Travo Group stands as a testament to engineering excellence across the Middle East, with operations spanning Lebanon, Oman, UAE, and expanding into Qatar.

Regional Excellence - Today
